About Jon

Jon is a 47 year old father and grandfather. He had a former career as a Mariner in the Gulf of Mexico, running crew boats and utility boats in the oilfield. He left his career to raise is son as a single parent. After moving home to Montana circumstances changed and he found himself starting college late in life. Jon earned an Associate of Arts at Bitteroot College as well as a certificate as a Computer Support Specialist, and COMP TIA A+ certification. He is currently a student of Media Arts at the University of Montana. He is also an honorably discharged veteran of the United States Navy, having served aboard the U.S.S. Blueridge L.C.C. 19 as an operation specialist. He currently holds a letter of continuity as a Merchant Marine Officer. Jon spent 14 years in the bottle a fact he is not proud of. He has been sober now for nearly 16 years. The world is a safer place due to this fact. Jon has little patience for organized religion or those that use it as a weapon. As an eclectic solitare he believes in tolerance.
